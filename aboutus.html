About Our Classes

Welcome to Thulir Arts And Crafts! I'm Revathi, a mom with a passion for art and a desire to share that passion with young minds. My online art classes provide a fun and engaging space for children to explore their creativity, learn new skills, and express themselves through art. I believe that every child is an artist, and my goal is to help them discover their own unique talents and abilities. In my classes, we'll experiment with a variety of materials and techniques, from drawing and painting to sculpting and mixed media. With small class sizes and a focus on individual growth, I strive to nurture each child's artistic journey in a supportive and encouraging environment.
